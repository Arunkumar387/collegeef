College Day

Celebrate the spirit of our college community on College Day! This annual event is a culmination of talent, creativity, and camaraderie, bringing together students, faculty, and staff for a day of festivities and entertainment. From cultural performances to awards ceremonies, College Day is a time to showcase our diverse talents and achievements while fostering a sense of pride and unity within the college.

Sports Day

Get ready to unleash your competitive spirit on Sports Day! This action-packed event is a celebration of athleticism, teamwork, and sportsmanship, featuring a variety of exciting games and competitions. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or a casual sports enthusiast, Sports Day offers something for everyone, from track and field events to team sports and friendly matches. Join us for a day of healthy competition and fun-filled activities!

Fresher's Day

Welcome to the college family on Fresher's Day! This special event is dedicated to welcoming new students and introducing them to the vibrant culture and traditions of our college. From orientation sessions to icebreaker activities, Fresher's Day is designed to help newcomers feel at home and connect with their peers. Join us as we embark on this exciting journey together and create memories that will last a lifetime!

Talent Show

Unleash your creativity and shine on stage at the Talent Show! This annual showcase is a platform for students to showcase their talents and passions, whether it's singing, dancing, acting, or any other form of artistic expression. With enthusiastic audiences and supportive peers, the Talent Show is a chance to celebrate individuality and diversity while inspiring others with your performance. Join us for a night of entertainment and inspiration!

Tours

Embark on exciting adventures and explore new horizons with our college Tours! Whether it's educational field trips, cultural excursions, or outdoor adventures, our Tours offer students valuable opportunities for learning, exploration, and personal growth. From historical landmarks to natural wonders, each tour is meticulously planned to provide enriching experiences and lasting memories. Join us as we journey together and discover the world beyond the classroom!

Celebrations

Join the Department Associations and immerse yourself in academic and extracurricular activities tailored to your field of study! These associations provide platforms for students to engage in research, discussions, workshops, and events related to their specific disciplines. From guest lectures by industry experts to hands-on projects and competitions, Department Associations offer valuable opportunities for learning, networking, and personal development. Join us and become an active member of your academic community!
